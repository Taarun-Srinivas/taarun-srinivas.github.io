About me

Hello! I am Taarun Srinivas, currently a graduate student at New York University (NYU), Tandon School of Engineeering, majoring in mechatronics and Robotics. My areas of interests are computer vision, deep learning, sensor fusion, SLAM, 3D Reconstruction, and Motion Planning.

Prior to my Master's, I was working as a software engineer at Siemens India, specializing in projects under computer vision and deep learning domains. I am passionate about these fields simply because I love viewing this world from a machine's perspective. Devoting my time and efforts to providing autonomy to machines and make them take intelligent decisions is a great challenge and that's what excites me to contribute to this field.

In my project endeavors, I prioritize the process over results, believing that this approach yields optimal outcomes. This philosophy has proven successful in my work while collaborating with diverse teams.

I thrive on collaborating with individuals from varied backgrounds and ethnicities, which has honed my communication skills and leadership qualities. While remaining composed in high-pressure situations, I concentrate on turning challenges into opportunities throughout my continuous learning journey.
